Who We Are

Craftsmanship
You Can Walk On

At Turquoise Flooring Inc., we believe a beautifully installed floor is the foundation of every great space. We install all types of wood floor — glue down, staple down, and floating systems — alongside laminate, cork, vinyl plank, tile, carpet, and polished concrete.

Every project starts with a conversation. We sit down with you, understand your vision, discuss materials, and plan every detail before a single plank is laid. Then comes the prep: cleaning, sanding, levelling, and patching the subfloor to ensure a flawless foundation. The result? Floors that exceed expectations — and clients whose satisfaction we never stop caring about.

We don't disappear after installation. Follow-up is part of our promise. We never hesitate to return a client's call — because our reputation is built on work that lasts.

How We Work

Our Installation Process

A proven four-step approach that ensures every project is delivered on time, on budget, and beyond expectation.

01

Consultation

We discuss your space, style preferences, budget, and timeline. Every detail is documented so nothing is left to chance.

02

Planning & Materials

We help you select the perfect flooring material and organize all supplies before work begins — zero surprises on install day.

03

Subfloor Preparation

Thorough cleaning, sanding, levelling, and patching creates a flawless base. This step is the secret behind a floor that lasts.

04

Installation & Follow-Up

Expert installation with a quality check at every row. After completion, we stay available — our work doesn't end at the door.
